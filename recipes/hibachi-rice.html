Hibachi Fried Rice

Description

Hibachi Fried rice is a common meal at hibachi places, where the chefs cook in front of you. Many wonder how the fried rice is made, so I'm going to demystify this with this Hibachi Fried Rice Recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked White Rice
  • 1 tablespoon Grapeseed Oil
  • 2 Large Eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Onions
  • 2 tablespoons grated Ginger
  • 2 tablespoons Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sesame Oil
  • 1 Scallion

Instructions

  1. Prepare White Rice
  2. Heat pan to high heat, then put Grapeseed Oil and Eggs on
  3. Once eggs are finished, ser them aside. Then add butter, and allow it to melt
  4. Once melted, add Onions to mixture.
  5. After 3-4 minutes, add Ginger, the cooked eggs, and rice back into the pan.
  6. Mix thuroughly, then push the rice into an even layer, covering the pan
  7. Spread Soy Sauce around the sesame oil around the rice, then mix it evenly.
  8. Once evenly spread, serve hot.
