Hibachi Fried Rice

Description

Hibachi Fried rice is a common meal at hibachi places, where the chefs cook in front of you. Many wonder how the fried rice is made, so I'm going to demystify this with this Hibachi Fried Rice Recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked White Rice
  • 1 tablespoon Grapeseed Oil
  • 2 Large Eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Onions
  • 2 tablespoons grated Ginger
  • 2 tablespoons Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sesame Oil
  • 1 Scallion

Instructions

  1. Prepare White Rice
  2. Cook Ground Beef and Garlic on skillet over Medium Heat
  3. Mix Soy Sauce, Brown Sugar, Sesame Oil, Ground Ginger, Red Pepper Flakes, and Pepper in a bowl
  4. Pour mixture from Step 3 over Ground Beef, then mix for a few minutes
  5. Put rice and the Ground beef into a serving bowl, then add Onions and Sesame Seeds for garnish
  6. Serve Fresh
