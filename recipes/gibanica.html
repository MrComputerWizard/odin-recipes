Gibanica

Description

Gibanica is a delicious food originating from Serbia, it is a wonderful mixture of egg, various cheeses, and phillo dough to create a savory meal that everyone can enjoy.

So here is my recipe for Gibanica!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounce package of Cream Cheese
  • 9 large eggs
  • 2 1/2 pounds large-curd Cottage Cheese
  • 1/2 pound Feta Cheese (optional)
  • 1 pound Filo Dough
  • 8 ounces (1 cup) melted unsalted butter
