Korean Beef Rice Bowls

Description

Korean beef is a delicious, easy to make recipe that everyone will enjoy. The recipe uses simple ingredients that can be found at any American grociery store.

So here is my recipe for Korean Beef Rice Bowls!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound 90% Lean Ground Beef
  • 3 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Reduced Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Sesame Oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon Ground Ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon Pepper
  • 2 cups hot White Rice
  • sliced Green Onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix cream cheese. Add three eggs at a time, then beat until even. Repeat until all eggs are in bowl
  2. Mix cottage and feta cheese into mixture, set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Fold two sheets of dough in half, then place in pan
  4. Lightly brush filo dough with melted butter, then spread around 1/2 cup of the cheese mixture evenly around the pan.
  5. Place three sheets of dough on top of the dough just spread
  6. Repeat Steps 4 and 5 a few more times until almost out of dough. Place the last three filo dough layers on top, and spread the butter.
  7. Cook in over for 1 hour or until golden brown.
  8. Let sit for 20 minutes, then serve.
